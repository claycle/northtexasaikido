## Saturday-Sunday March 28-29, 2026

Patricia Hendricks Shihan will be returning to North Texas Aikido in Spring 2026.

Reserve your spot on the North Texas Aikido mat with Patricia Hendricks Shihan on Saturday and Sunday, March 28-29, 2026.

$130 for both days, Saturday and Sunday  
$90 for single days, Saturday or Sunday  
$50 for special Kaeshiwaza class on Sunday morning (supplemental fee)

**No observers will be allowed in the special Kaeshiwaza class. Only students who have purchased the supplement will be allowed into the dojo at this time.**

Check-in/Registration begins at 9AM each day. First session begins at 10AM. Sessions will end around 4PM. There will be several breaks.

_There will be a pot luck dinner party at the dojo immediately following Session 4 on Saturday, March 28, 2026._
